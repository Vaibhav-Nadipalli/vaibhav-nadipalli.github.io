Biography

I'm currently a high school student obsessed with physics, engineering and tech. I am yet to find what I really want to work on but am interested in a large range of everything tech, from spacecraft to consumer tech.

My journey started with binge-watching MKBHD videos, which sparked my interest in tech. This led me to discover how fascinating physics can be. The real turning point was reading "Sapiens" by Yuval Noah Harari. This book helped me to connect the dots between all the irrelevant things I learned at school and inspired me to make a positive impact.

I realised that the best way to achieve this is through business, and now my goal is to become a tech startup founder.
